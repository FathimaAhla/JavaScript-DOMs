Frequently Asked Questions

What is Information technology industry
An information technology industry, or IT industry, is any business model based on the collection, processing, distribution and use of information. Most industries use information technology to a significant extent. At some point, an old industry becomes so technology intensive that it begins to resemble an IT industry.
